## Introduction

Kinematics is the study of motion without considering its causes.

### Key Concepts

1. Position
2. Velocity
3. Acceleration

## Basic Equations

For constant acceleration:

$$
v = u + at
$$

$$
s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2
$$
